Our story
How It Started

Boltbite was born in 2019 out of a simple frustration: why does fast food have to taste like it was made in a hurry? Our founders grew up eating at the kinds of places where the burger actually tasted like beef and the fries came out hot every single time.

We set out to build something different — a fast food joint with the speed people needed and the quality they deserved. No frozen patties. No shortcuts. Every smash burger pressed to order, every shake hand-spun, every basket of fries pulled fresh from the fryer.

Six years later, Boltbite has grown to 47 locations across the country — but the mission hasn't changed one bit. Real fast. Real good. Every single time.

What drives us
Our Values
01
Fresh, Always
We use fresh beef, never frozen. Every patty is pressed on the griddle when you order — not sitting in a warming tray waiting for you.
02
Speed Without Sacrifice
Fast doesn't have to mean bad. Our kitchen is built around getting great food to you in under 12 minutes without cutting a single corner.
03
Community First
Every Boltbite location is a part of its neighborhood. We hire local, source local where we can, and give back to the communities we serve.
04
Bold Flavors
Every sauce, seasoning, and ingredient on our menu was developed in-house. We don't do bland. If it's not hitting, it's not on the menu.
05
Transparency
We put our full ingredient list online. No mystery meat, no hidden additives. You always know exactly what's going into your food.
06
Constant Improvement
We never stop tweaking. Every season we revisit our recipes, listen to customer feedback, and push to make every bite better than the last.
